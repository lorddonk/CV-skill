# Target Field Prioritization

Use this guide to decide what to emphasize based on the student's application direction.

## Computer Science / Data Science / AI

Emphasize:

- Programming languages, algorithms, data structures, software development.
- AI, machine learning, deep learning, NLP, computer vision, data mining.
- Research projects, coding projects, GitHub, open-source, competitions, technical tools.
- Tools such as Python, SQL, Java, C++, R, TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, MATLAB.

Prefer bullets that show technical implementation, model evaluation, dataset handling, system design, performance optimization, or measurable results.

## Business / Management / Finance / Economics

Emphasize:

- Internships, market research, financial modelling, data analysis, consulting projects.
- Leadership, teamwork, communication, strategic thinking, business impact.
- Competitions, case analysis, investment research, valuation, risk analysis.
- Tools such as Excel, SQL, Python, R, Stata, SPSS, Tableau, Power BI, Bloomberg.

Prefer bullets that show analysis, decision support, business insight, stakeholder communication, and measurable impact.

## Education

Emphasize:

- Teaching experience, curriculum design, student support, tutoring, classroom observation.
- Education research, survey design, qualitative/quantitative research methods.
- Communication, empathy, assessment design, learning outcomes.

Prefer bullets that show instructional design, student engagement, measurable learning improvement, and education-related research.

## Social Science / Public Policy / Communication

Emphasize:

- Research methods, survey design, interviews, policy analysis, literature review.
- Qualitative and quantitative analysis, writing, critical thinking, data interpretation.
- Social impact, community work, public communication, stakeholder analysis.

Prefer bullets that show research question, methodology, evidence interpretation, and policy or social relevance.

## Engineering

Emphasize:

- Technical projects, lab experience, modelling, simulation, design, testing, manufacturing.
- Practical outcomes, system performance, prototypes, reliability, optimization.
- Tools such as MATLAB, Simulink, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, ANSYS, Python, C/C++.

Prefer bullets that show engineering design process, experimental validation, technical constraints, and measurable performance.

## Interdisciplinary Applications

When the target direction is interdisciplinary, combine priorities. For example, fintech should balance finance evidence and technical/data skills; educational technology should balance education evidence and product/data/technical ability.
